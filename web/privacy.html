Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 26, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Watermark Remover ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Device Information: We collect a unique device identifier (Installation ID) to manage your free usage quota and in-app purchases.
  • Images: Images you select for editing are temporarily processed on our servers and are not stored permanently. We do not access, view, or retain your images after processing is complete.
  • Purchase Information: When you make in-app purchases, Apple processes the transaction. We only receive a receipt to verify your purchase status.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide our watermark removal service
  • To track your free usage quota
  • To verify and manage your in-app purchases
  • To improve our service quality

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your Installation ID is stored securely on your device using encrypted storage.
  • Images are processed in memory and are not permanently stored on our servers.
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect data during transmission.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For in-app purchases and app distribution
  • Cloud Processing: For image processing (images are processed but not stored)

6. Data Retention

  • Device identifiers are retained as long as you use the app.
  • Images are deleted immediately after processing.
  • Purchase records are retained for account management and support purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data by contacting us
  • Uninstall the app to remove locally stored data
  • Contact us with questions about your data

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
